Developing Identifiers for Heritage Collections

This resource describes the different aspects of developing persistent identifiers (PIDs) in the context of Heritage Collections. It is now available in an initial version for consultation and feedback until 7 June 2021.

Before you begin...

This resource is aimed at senior technical stakeholders within galleries, libraries, archives and museums to assist with developing requirements and advocacy for new services. Have a look at the Read Me section before getting started.
